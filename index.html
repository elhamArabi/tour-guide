Visit Stockholm

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and artistic designs that this charming city has to offer. From world-renowned museums and galleries to stunning architectural landmarks, there's no shortage of opportunities to explore and appreciate the local art scene.

Top three activities to do at Stockholm

Vasa Museum

The Vasa ship capsized and sank in Stockholm 1628. After 333 years on the seabed the mighty warship was salvaged and the voyage could continue. Today Vasa is the world's only preserved 17th century ship.

Gamla Stan (Old Town)

The Old Town, unsurprisingly, is the oldest part of Stockholm. It's certainly the tourist's go-to destination, but that doesn’t detract from its architectural and otherwise urban beauty.

ABBA The Museum

ABBA The Museum is more than an ordinary museum. Naturally, the members’ costumes, gold records, original items, memorabilia, and much, much more are here. But at this museum you are an important part of the experience!
